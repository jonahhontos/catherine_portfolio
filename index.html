Golden Sand Slots is a social mobile casino app for IOS and Android  with over 500,000 users by Monarc Gaming Labs. I worked in house to create the music and sound effects for over 30 of the slot machines within the app as well as interface sounds, bangup tunes, and sound for promotional videos. All of the sound in this app had to be mono and as short as possible to keep file size down. Writing in a new genre every week really stretched my composition chops.

Whispering Willows is an indie horror game for Ouya, PC, and Mac by Nightlight Interactive. It's won several awards including the Ouya Create Game Jam prize for Most Immersive.  I worked with our programmer to implement sounds and with our composer, Steve Goldshein, to create a unified sonic experience between the music and the sound effects. I designed and programmed the dynamic music system that controls the addition, removal, and syncing of music stems in reaction to game events. We had a team of testers and I was responsible for squashing audio bugs and coaching the testers on how to find and report audio issues. I was responsible for co-ordinating with and directing the voice actress for Elena, Skylar Davenport. I also took care of all other loose ends with audio of this game.

My roles: Audio Director, Sound Design, Audio Implementation, Dynamic music system design and programming

Whispering Willows review by Hardcore Gamer  - “The sound design is fantastic and relies heavily on atmosphere…the game is meant to make you feel uneasy whenever possible, everything about the audio succeeds at accomplishing that goal.” Whispering Willows review by Nerdy But Flirty - “The music and sound design is also very good, with the creaks of the mansion and skitters of things in the walls contributing to a vaguely unsettling atmosphere.” Whispering Willows review by Gameramble - “Thanks to the haunting music and excellent sound effects I found myself frequently stopping just to admire the environments and soak in the atmosphere.”

Bust N Rush is a 3D action runner from Techtonic Games. The music is a mix of hard rock and electronica with a layered, looping music system that increases the musical intensity alongside the action of the game. The sound has a ton of explosions and impacts so it uses a dynamic volume adjusting system to ensure that it's as intense as possible at all times.

My roles: Sound Design, Music, Audio Programming (all audio)

Alien Hero is a 2D runner by Apptivus with a whole lot ‘o blood and gore. I proactively leveraged cutting edge fruit smashing paradigms and integrated many dog food smooshing synergies. I made a very large quantity of playdough and threw it at various surfaces to make all the disgusting human body part sounds. I composed, produced, and performed the progressive rock sound track which features real meat space guitar and bass guitar.

My Roles: Sound Design, Music (all audio content creation)

About Me

I do everything audio related for games including sound effects, music, implementation, sound for promotional videos, and just about everything else. As far as musical style I write whatever style fits, but I tend toward electronica that’s heavily inspired by early video game music, progressive rock, 80’s pop, metal, and trance. For sound design, I create absolutely every sound that I can from scratch. I love designing synths and I love processing the living bejesus out of a sound to create something new and unreal. I’ve taught myself programming so that I can create interactive and dynamic music and sound systems. I have a BA in Recording Arts from California State University, Chico and I like bass guitar, prog rock, Sonic the Hedgehog, Minecraft, rock climbing, chicken burritos, and kitties.
